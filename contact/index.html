FAQs
Q1: What types of healing sessions do you offer?
We offer personalized, one-on-one healing sessions both online and in person. Each session is tailored to address individual needs using energy and holistic methods.

Q2: How can I book a session?
You can book a session through our website or by contacting us directly. We recommend scheduling in advance as slots fill up quickly.

Q3: What is the mode of payment for sessions?
Currently, we accept cash payments only. For in-person sessions, please bring the payment with you. For online sessions, payment arrangements will be discussed at the time of booking.

Q4: How long is each session, and what can I expect?
Each session typically lasts between 30-45 minutes. We will start with a brief discussion of your goals, followed by the healing work, and conclude with guidance for post-session practices.

Q5: Do I need any prior experience with healing to benefit from these sessions?
No prior experience is necessary. Our methods are accessible to everyone, whether you’re new to healing or have previous experience with similar practices.

Q6: Are online healing sessions as effective as in-person ones?
Yes, online healing sessions can be just as effective. Energy healing transcends physical distance, allowing us to work together regardless of location.

Q7: How many sessions will I need?
The number of sessions depends on your individual needs and goals. We will assess your progress and make recommendations based on what will benefit you most.

Q8: What should I do to prepare for my session?
Find a comfortable, quiet space where you won’t be disturbed, and be open to the experience. If possible, wear comfortable clothing and have water available.

What happens in the session
